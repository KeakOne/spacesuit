About

Spacesuit specializes in importing on-trend but definitely not mainstream fashion for our target market of18-30 year olds. We also import hard to find 1990s retro with an emphasis on menswear. Our retro and vintage clothing originates in the USA, Japan, Europe and Korea

At Spacesuit We believe in providing REAL VALUE to our customers. This is reflected in our great pricing and various in-store deals. Spacesuit is also a green business with 80% recycled and up-cycled retro clothing and accessories. We hand pick each and every item at source overseas to ensure quality and uniqueness. We do not buy anything online.
